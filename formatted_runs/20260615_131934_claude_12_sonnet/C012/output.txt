External Credit Score Retrieval And Fallback Handling

core_business_functionality: |
This chunk retrieves an applicant's credit score from the external credit
bureau service using their national identification number. It applies a
default score when the bureau has no record, when the response is malformed,
or when the returned score cannot be interpreted, while treating outright
communication failures as a hard error.

business_function: |
A credit score is requested from the external credit bureau using the
applicant's national identification number.
A default credit score is used when the bureau has no credit history for the
applicant.
A default credit score is used when the bureau's response does not contain a
usable score.
A request without a national identification number cannot proceed to a credit
check.
A request that the credit bureau outright rejects results in the credit check
being treated as unavailable.
