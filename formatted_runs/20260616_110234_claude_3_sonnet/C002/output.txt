business_functional_name: |
Loan Application Submission Request Capture

core_business_functionality: |
This component defines and enforces the structure of the information a
borrower or originating officer must supply when submitting a new loan
application, including all field-level constraints that enforce business
eligibility rules at the point of entry. It ensures that only well-formed,
policy-compliant application data reaches internal processing.

business_function: |
Capture the unique identifier of the borrower submitting the application.
Enforce that the borrower identifier follows the institution's standard
applicant reference format.
Capture the monetary amount the borrower wishes to borrow.
Enforce that the requested loan amount falls within the institution's
permitted lending range.
Capture the number of months over which the borrower wishes to repay.
Enforce that the requested repayment duration falls within the institution's
approved tenure boundaries.
Capture the category of loan product being applied for.
Capture the borrower's stated reason for seeking the loan.
Limit the length of the stated loan purpose to prevent overly long
submissions.
Capture the originating branch code where the application is being processed.
Enforce that the branch code conforms to the permitted length range.
Capture the identifier of the relationship manager sponsoring the application.
Enforce that the relationship manager identifier follows the institution's
standard RM reference format.
Capture the borrower's declaration that they have no existing loan default.
Capture the borrower's self-reported gross monthly income.
Enforce that declared monthly income is within the permissible reporting
range.
Capture the borrower's self-reported total monthly debt obligations.
Enforce that declared monthly obligations are within the permissible reporting
range.
