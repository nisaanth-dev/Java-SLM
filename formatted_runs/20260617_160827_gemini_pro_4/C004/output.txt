business_functional_name: |
Core Claim Processing And Routing

core_business_functionality: |
Executes the central business logic for evaluating, recording, and routing
newly submitted insurance claims. Applies financial thresholds to determine
immediate status and publishes creation events to downstream systems.

  - Orchestrates the ingestion of a new claim record.
  - Evaluates financial thresholds to assign initial processing status.
  - Persists evaluated claim records to the database.
  - Publishes notifications for newly recorded claims.
  - Retrieves the current state of an existing claim.
