business_functional_name: |
Claim Data Translation

core_business_functionality: |
Translates data between the format used for external communication and the
internal database structure. Ensures safe mapping of attributes and safely
extracts identifiers from related document records.

business_function: 
  - Converts incoming submission requests into internal database records.
  - Safely handles missing or empty incoming requests.
  - Converts internal database records into outbound response messages.
  - Extracts lists of attached document identifiers from related records.
  - Supplies empty lists when no supporting documents are attached.

  - Convert Request
  - Handle Missing Input
  - Convert Record
  - Extract Identifiers
  - Supply Defaults
