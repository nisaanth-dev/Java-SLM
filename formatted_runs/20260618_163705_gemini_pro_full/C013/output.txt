business_functional_name: |
Administrative Claim Override Management

core_business_functionality: |
Provides privileged operational capabilities to manually alter the processing
trajectory of an insurance claim. Allows authorized personnel to force a claim
into an approved or rejected state directly, bypassing standard automated
workflows.

business_function: 
  - Facilitates manual authorization of a claim by administrative personnel.
  - Secures the approval process by validating the presence of a claim identifier.
  - Facilitates manual rejection of a claim by administrative personnel.
  - Mandates the inclusion of a justification reason when a claim is manually rejected.

  - Authorize Claim
  - Validate Identifier
  - Reject Claim
  - Mandate Justification
