business_functional_name: |
Claim Lifecycle Event Auditing

core_business_functionality: |
Subscribes to the central messaging system to silently observe and record
significant changes in a claim's lifecycle, ensuring a durable, read-only
audit trail is maintained without interfering with primary business
operations.

business_function: 
  - Listens to the enterprise message broker for claim events.
  - Discards empty or blank event notifications without processing.
  - Records the exact content of the event payload to the permanent audit trail.
  - Captures and logs processing failures to prevent the background listener thread from crashing.
