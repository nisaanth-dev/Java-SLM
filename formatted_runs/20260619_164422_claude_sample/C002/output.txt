business_functional_name: |
Loan Application Lifecycle Management And Event Dispatch

core_business_functionality: |
This component is the authoritative business logic centre for all loan
application operations, responsible for validating, persisting, and tracking
the full lifecycle of a loan from initial submission through to final status.
It enforces the organisation's rules governing permissible status transitions
and business data integrity, and signals significant lifecycle events to
downstream consumers upon application creation.

business_function: |
A new loan application is validated against the organisation's business rules,
assigned a unique reference identifier, persisted in the system of record, and
a lifecycle event is dispatched to notify downstream systems of the
submission.
A single loan application is retrieved by its unique reference, with an
explicit failure raised when no matching record exists.
All loan applications belonging to a specific applicant are retrieved as a
complete collection and converted into the standard response representation.
The recorded status of a loan application is advanced to a specified new value
after confirming the transition is permitted, with the approval timestamp
captured when the application moves to approved standing.
The current status of a loan application is retrieved in isolation for
lightweight inspection without loading full application data.
Submitted loan data is examined against mandatory field requirements â
amount, term, and stated purpose â and rejected with a precise explanation
when any requirement is unmet.
